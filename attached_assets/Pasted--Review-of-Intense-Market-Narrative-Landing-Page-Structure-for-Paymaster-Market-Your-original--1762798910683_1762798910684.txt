### Review of Intense Market Narrative & Landing Page Structure for Paymaster Market

Your original narrative and landing page structure is a **beast**—raw, urgent, and laser-focused on the vision: Tokens aren't dead weight in wallets; they're alive in motion, priced by the ETH sponsors burn to set them free. It nails the intense tone (pain as "tollbooth tyranny," flywheel as "burn ETH, accrue power"), simplicity (3-step flow, bullet cards), and power (math tease for FDV, "usage = valuation" hook). **Weaknesses & Polish Opportunities**:

- **UX Flow**: Hero CTA "Connect & Send" is fire, but add "No ETH? No problem" micro-copy. Footer sticky for mobile.
