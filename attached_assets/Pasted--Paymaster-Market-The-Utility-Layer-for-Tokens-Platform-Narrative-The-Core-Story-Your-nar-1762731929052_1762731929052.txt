# Paymaster Market: The Utility Layer for Tokens

## **Platform Narrative: The Core Story**
Your narrative should hit like a manifesto—raw, unapologetic, zero fluff. Frame it as **the rebellion against ETH's tyranny**: Tokens aren't meant to rot in wallets; they're meant to *move*. Paymaster Market doesn't just fix gas— it **prices tokens by their real-world pulse**. Sponsors bet ETH on a token's future sends, earning a slice of the action. Users send like breathing. Rebalancers feast on the spreads. Over time, the system spits out an "intended marketcap": ETH burned for usability vs. tokens accrued as fees, scaled to supply. It's not speculation—it's **usage as valuation**.

**One-Line Elevator Pitch**: "Send any token gasless. Earn yield from its traction. Watch it price itself."

**Tone**: Intense (bold claims, scarcity vibe—"Join before the flywheel spins"). Simple (short sentences, active voice). Powerful (metaphors: "ETH as invisible rail; tokens as living economies").

**Narrative Arc** (for copy across page):
1. **Hook**: The Pain (ETH locks tokens away).
2. **Reveal**: The Fix (Pools turn burn into yield).
3. **Ignite**: The Flywheel (Usage → Fees → Arbs → Implied Caps).
4. **Close**: The Call (Be a sponsor. Send now. Arb the edge).

## **Landing Page Structure: Hero to Horizon**
Build for conversion: Mobile-first, <5s load, one scroll tells the story. Use Figma/Webflow for prototype—dark mode default, neon accents (ETH green, token blues). Hero video: 10s loop of a token "unlocking" from a wallet, flying gasless to another.

- **Visual**: Full-bleed animation—ETH "tollbooth" crumbles; tokens cascade freely. Subtle FDV ticker: "Tokens priced by usage: $DOGGO implied cap: 2.5M ETH ↑".
- **Headline** (H1, 72pt bold): **Tokens Die in Wallets. Revive Them.**
- **Subheadline** (24pt): Send any token gasless. Earn yield from every transfer. Price its true worth.
- **CTA Buttons** (Dual, stacked):
  - Primary: **[Connect & Send]** (Wallet popup → gasless demo with held tokens).
  - Secondary: **[Sponsor a Pool]** (Form: Token + ETH deposit).
- **Micro-Proof**: "1M+ transfers. $5M ETH sponsored. 25% avg APY."

*Why Intense/Simple/Powerful*: Zero explanation—pure provocation. User feels the lock-in, sees the escape.

### **4. How It Works: "The Flywheel in 3 Steps" (Scroll 3)**

- **Headline** (H2): **Burn ETH. Accrue Power.**
- **Step-by-Step (Numbered, Icon-Driven)**:
  1. **Sponsor**: Deposit ETH. Choose $DOGGO. Fee: 0.5%. Min: 10 tokens. *"Bet on its sends."*
  2. **Send**: User transfers 100 $DOGGO gasless. Pays 0.5 fee. You cover gas. *"Tokens flow free."*
  3. **Evolve**: Fees pile up. ETH burns down. Ratio implies FDV: 2M ETH. Arbs tighten it. *"Usage prices the cap."*

- **Math Tease** (Subtle, Click-Expand):  
  > Implied FDV = (ETH Burned / Tokens Accrued) × Supply  
  > $DOGGO: 500 ETH / 200K tokens × 1B supply = 2.5M ETH (~$10M). *Live tracker below.*

- **Interactive Demo**: Embed mini-simulator—Input token/supply, mock transfers → Watch FDV rise.

*Why Intense*: Steps as commands ("Bet. Flow. Price."). Simple: 3 beats. Powerful: Reveals the "secret sauce" without jargon.
